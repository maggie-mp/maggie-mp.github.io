Engoo Interface


The aim of this design was to create a whiteboard interface that is intuitive to use, has a simplistic layout and a variety of visual tools. It should follow Jacob's Law of following design nuances found in other whiteboard interfaces and holds up against competitors. It should function and cater to tutors and students who need the interface to help visualise learnings from English lessons.
