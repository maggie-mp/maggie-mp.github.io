Services

I have the skills to help YOU achieve your graphic design dreams!


What will you get from me?

  • Custom Designs – Don't know what you want? Not a problem! Whether you come in with lots of ideas and requirements or have no idea where to start, I am more than happy to create a design that bests suits your needs!

  • Open Communication – Responses to your questions will be answered quickly, within three working days!

  • Variety – I will give you a range of different styles and variations to ideas, concepts and even final designs to choose from! If you have a certain style you want me to take inspiration from- feel free to reach out to me on my contacts section!

Design Software used on-site:

  • Photoshop – This well known programme allows editing of photos and the creation of digital artwork for your graphic design needs.

  • Illustrator – This software is good for logos, illustrations and vector files! This has helped me create products like logos and signage.

  • InDesign – This is a multi-page layout programme which is used for physical programmes and pamphlets.

  • After Effects – I recently learnt this programme through my degree, I have learnt how to create interesting motion graphics.

  • Sublime – This programme is used to create HTML and CSS files for website design.

About

Hello awesome human! My name is Maggie McMillan-Perry but most people call me Mags! I am a graphics designer based in Featherston, New Zealand. I have been creating graphic design projects seriously for about 3 years. I have a solid background in marketing, brand design, visual design and HTML + CSS coding. I am currently studying at Massey University, doing a Bachelor of Design with Honours, majoring in Visual Communication Design! I have always been interested in learning new things since I was young. Alongside designing graphic products for clients, I have a keen interest in music and compostion which I have found useful for clients when original music is needed as part of a project.
